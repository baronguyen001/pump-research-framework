# Disclaimer

This repository is educational research and software. It is not financial,
investment, tax, legal, or accounting advice. It does not recommend buying,
selling, shorting, farming, staking, or holding any asset.

`pumpscore` is an offline rule engine over numbers you enter by hand. It does
not fetch market data, use private wallet lists, call paid APIs, hold secrets,
or provide automated trading signals.

Case studies are selected examples from public market history. They are not a
representative sample of all tokens. For every visible winner, many more tokens
failed, rugged, dumped, or stopped trading. Backtests and historical examples do
not predict future returns.

Crypto assets are volatile and can go to zero. Use independent research, local
professional advice, and strict risk limits.
