# Risk Management

## Wallet hygiene

- Keep long-term capital in cold storage.
- Use a separate hot wallet for experimental contracts.
- Avoid unlimited approvals on new contracts.
- Review old approvals with tools such as revoke.cash.
- Bookmark important sites and avoid wallet links from direct messages.

## Venue risk

- Do not keep all liquid capital on one exchange.
- Watch spread and withdrawal reliability, not only headline liquidity.
- Stablecoin risk is not zero; understand issuer, chain, and bridge exposure.

## Anti-FOMO toolkit

- 24-hour rule: when a token feels urgent, wait one day unless the strategy is
  explicitly built for launch speed.
- Pre-commit size: write size before opening the order screen.
- Timeline break: stop reading social feeds for 30 minutes before a decision.
- Buffett question: if the token drops 50 percent tomorrow, would you buy more
  from conviction or panic?

## Psychology checks

- Recency bias: one big win is not a new base rate.
- Sunk cost: a large loss is not a reason to hold.
- Echo chamber: following only holders hides red flags.
- Hindsight: "I knew it" is not a trading log.

## Ready for 3 percent or more?

Before a position reaches 3 percent of bankroll:

- [ ] I can explain the thesis in two sentences.
- [ ] I know the invalidation.
- [ ] I checked tokenomics and unlocks.
- [ ] I checked liquidity and slippage.
- [ ] I wrote the exit plan.
- [ ] I can tolerate a full loss of this amount.
- [ ] I am not increasing size because of FOMO.

If any item fails, reduce size or skip.
