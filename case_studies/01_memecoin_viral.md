# Pattern A: Memecoin Viral

SURVIVORSHIP-BIAS CAVEAT: these are selected winners. Most memecoins fail. The
lesson is not "buy memes"; it is "understand why viral reflexivity sometimes
overpowers traditional signals."

## SHIB

- Chain: Ethereum.
- Approximate window: 2021.
- Approximate multiple: around 1000x from early levels.
- Public triggers: meme reflexivity, exchange access, high-profile attention,
  and the public burn narrative.
- Layers that helped: social and catalyst.
- Layers that did not help enough: traditional on-chain quality would have been
  noisy and late.

## PEPE

- Chain: Ethereum.
- Approximate window: April to May 2023.
- Approximate multiple: several hundred times from early trading.
- Public triggers: simple meme, fast X spread, quick CEX listings.
- Layers that helped: social velocity and catalyst.
- Layers that failed: anti-FOMO rules would have rejected it after the first
  explosive leg.

## BONK

- Chain: Solana.
- Approximate window: late 2022 to early 2023.
- Approximate multiple: roughly 100x from depressed early trading.
- Public triggers: Solana community airdrop during post-FTX pessimism.
- Layers that helped: narrative reversal and social community identity.
- Layers that failed: macro filters would have said Solana was too damaged.

## WIF

- Chain: Solana.
- Approximate window: late 2023 to early 2024.
- Approximate multiple: very large, often cited around 1000x to 2000x from deep
  lows.
- Public triggers: simple image, Solana memecoin rotation, cult-like social
  spread, exchange access.
- Layers that helped: social.
- Layers that failed: the move was too fast for cautious on-chain confirmation.

## POPCAT

- Chain: Solana.
- Approximate window: 2024.
- Approximate multiple: large memecoin winner from early levels.
- Public triggers: memecoin launchpad rotation, Solana attention, CEX listings.
- Layers that helped: narrative and social.
- Layers that failed: valuation discipline arrived late.

## Pattern summary

Memecoin winners usually have a simple image, organic mid-tier spread, chain
timing, and exchange access. They also violate many careful rules. The safer
research conclusion is to cap memecoin exposure, not to chase every viral chart.
