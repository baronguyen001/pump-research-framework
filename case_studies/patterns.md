# Patterns and Red Flags

This is the canonical checklist mirrored by `pumpscore checklist`.

## 10 repeating patterns

- [ ] `hot_narrative_early`: narrative is hot in crypto-native circles before
  mainstream coverage.
- [ ] `small_starting_mcap`: market cap is still small enough for asymmetric
  upside.
- [ ] `healthy_holder_distribution`: top holder concentration is reasonable.
- [ ] `liquidity_growing`: DEX liquidity is above a usable floor and increasing.
- [ ] `volume_mcap_accumulation`: volume to market cap stays elevated.
- [ ] `smart_money_present`: public wallet research shows skilled wallets or
  funds present.
- [ ] `organic_mid_tier_social`: mid-tier social accounts discuss it without
  obvious paid-promo patterns.
- [ ] `tier2_before_tier1`: tier-2 exchange access exists before tier-1 listing.
- [ ] `active_development`: the team or developer community is visibly shipping.
- [ ] `clear_30_90d_catalyst`: a public catalyst sits inside 30 to 90 days.

Default interpretation:

- 8 or more patterns and zero red flags: strong research case.
- 6 or more patterns and zero red flags: watchlist.
- Any red flag: caution.
- Fewer than 6 patterns: pass.

## 7 red flags

- [ ] `top10_over_50pct`: top 10 holders control more than 50 percent outside
  known venues or burn addresses.
- [ ] `liquidity_unlocked`: liquidity is unlocked or lock duration is too short.
- [ ] `anon_team_sketchy_funding`: anonymous team plus poor funding quality.
- [ ] `single_venue_wash_volume`: volume is concentrated on one venue or looks
  wash-like.
- [ ] `large_unlock_or_emissions`: large unlock or emissions arrive soon.
- [ ] `already_50x_week`: token already moved more than 50x in less than a week.
- [ ] `paid_promo_social`: social spread is mostly paid accounts or spam.

The checklist is not a model. It is a way to force the research notes into a
shape that can be challenged later.
