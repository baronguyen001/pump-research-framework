# Pattern B: L1 and Infrastructure Narratives

SURVIVORSHIP-BIAS CAVEAT: infrastructure winners are easier to explain after
they win. Many funded chains never reached durable usage.

## SOL

- Category: high-throughput L1.
- Approximate window: 2020 to 2021.
- Approximate multiple: hundreds of times from early cycle lows.
- Public triggers: "Ethereum alternative" narrative, ecosystem funding, NFT
  activity, exchange access.
- Layers that helped: narrative, catalyst, and on-chain usage.
- Layers that did not help enough: concentration and sponsor risk were easy to
  underestimate.

## AVAX

- Category: L1 and subnet ecosystem.
- Approximate window: 2021.
- Approximate multiple: around 70x from early-cycle levels.
- Public triggers: subnet narrative, ecosystem incentives, DeFi growth.
- Layers that helped: narrative and on-chain TVL.
- Layers that did not help enough: incentive-driven TVL can leave quickly.

## MATIC

- Category: Ethereum scaling.
- Approximate window: 2021.
- Approximate multiple: very large cycle winner.
- Public triggers: L2 scaling narrative, partnerships, developer adoption.
- Layers that helped: narrative, catalyst, and usage.
- Layers that did not help enough: high beta to the broader cycle.

## TIA

- Category: modular blockchain.
- Approximate window: late 2023 to early 2024.
- Approximate multiple: around 10x in the first major move.
- Public triggers: modular narrative, airdrop distribution, developer attention.
- Layers that helped: narrative and catalyst.
- Layers that did not help enough: unlock schedules still mattered.

## ICP counter-case

- Category: infrastructure launch.
- Approximate window: 2021 launch.
- Public outcome: strong launch hype followed by a severe drawdown.
- Lesson: a strong narrative and major listing can still fail if valuation,
  supply, unlocks, and market timing are poor.

## Pattern summary

Infrastructure moves are more compatible with the four-layer model than pure
memecoins. Look for usage, developer activity, and incentives that outlast the
subsidy cycle.
