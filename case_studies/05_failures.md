# Failures and Reality Check

SURVIVORSHIP-BIAS CAVEAT: this file is as important as the winners. Avoiding
bad trades is part of the edge.

## ICP

ICP had major launch attention and exchange access, then suffered a severe
drawdown. The lesson is that listing quality and technical ambition do not
override valuation, unlocks, and distribution.

## Airdrop dumps

LayerZero and EigenLayer are useful counter-cases for airdrop farming. When
recipients see the token as a payout and there is limited post-claim demand,
listing day can become distribution.

## AI-agent collapse

The 2024 AI-agent wave showed real creativity and real reflexivity. It also
showed how quickly a category can fall when attention rotates away and token
valuation lacks cash flow support.

## Memecoin launchpad base rate

Public reports around memecoin launchpads repeatedly show a very low share of
tokens reaching durable liquidity. Even when the exact graduation rate changes
by source and date, the practical lesson is stable: random spray-and-pray has a
terrible base rate.

## 2025-2026 maturity check

The market is more mature than 2021. Narratives still move, but capital is more
selective, supply is larger, and obvious meta trades crowd faster. A framework
should help avoid poor setups at least as much as it helps find winners.

## Practical takeaways

- Cap memecoin exposure as a basket, not as a conviction position.
- Treat unlocks and emissions as first-class risk.
- Do not confuse a points campaign with durable demand.
- Record misses. A strategy without miss logs is a story, not a process.
